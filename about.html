Kendra is a Salesforce Developer driven by a passion for building innovative solutions and empowering the next generation of engineers. With over 5 years of experience in front-end, back-end, and integrations, she specializes in designing and building applications for business technology users. Her journey began in consulting, where she designed and implemented custom Salesforce solutions for clients in diverse industries like Higher Education and Technology. Currently at YouTube, Kendra focuses on optimizing partner-facing teams through actionable insights and automation.

Committed to increasing representation in tech, Kendra actively shares her knowledge and experience with aspiring Black Salesforce Developers. She recently co-led a Google Code Next Connect club, introducing students to the fundamentals of web development and design.
